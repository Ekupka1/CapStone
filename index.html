Who are we?
A Computer Science Club


We are the Cornell College computer science club. We strive to hone our skills and create a strong friendly environment. We host fun events like a annual hackathon, server tour, and movie nights! We want to build a community to hance our computer science skills where you can present your latest project and get healthy feed back!

Where? 218 West Science Hall

Time? 3:30pm - 4:30pm
